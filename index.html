About Sparrow Guardian

We focus on protecting sparrows and rebuilding their habitats in urban and rural environments.

Our Mission

Our mission is to protect sparrow populations by improving nesting spaces, reducing pollution, and raising public awareness.

Once common in cities worldwide, sparrows now face habitat loss and food shortages.

Through research, community action, and sustainable urban planning, we work to bring sparrows back.
